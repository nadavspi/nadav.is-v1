Friday the 13th

December 13, 2013

It’s Friday the 13th, “an unlucky day in Western and Eastern superstition” according to Wikipedia.

Thelonious Sphere Monk wrote a tune called Friday the 13th. Like all of his tunes, it’s quirky and swings hard.
